A Brief about Finance

Finance is a broad term that is used to define the management of money. It is what deals with the questions of how the individuals, consumers and the country as a whole spend and save money. Thus, Investments and savings are highly integral terms in this world of finance. The term, finance is classified into three major categories: Personal finance, corporate finance and public finance each of which provides diverse functions when segregated.

While public finance deals with the money that is spent by the countries, municipalities as a whole, personal finance is quite the contrary. It is concerned with the money spent by individuals. Corporate finance is thus the source of funding and the capital structure for corporations. All three categories of finance are very important in their respective fields. Homework help finance can assist you in learning more about this.
