Basic requirements for becoming an Online Tutor

  • Subject Knowledge: - You must have excellent command over your specialization (academic subjects). Prior experience in teaching would be given weightage.
  • Laptop or PC: - You must have computer or laptop to provide your services of online tutoring and homework assignment help with knowledge of MS Word, Excel and Powerpoint.
  • Digital Pen Tablet: - Digital pen Tablet is mandatory for online tutoring, as it is required to teach online on our web based tutoring platform. For homework assignment job, it is not needed.
  • Broadband internet Connection: - You must have broadband internet connection (256 kbps or higher) for online tutoring because it is basic requirement to run our web based application (White board).
  • Good Communication Skills: - For online tutoring and homework assignment job, you must have very good verbal and written communication skills. Plagiarism would not be accepted at any cost.
